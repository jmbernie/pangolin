What is a pangolin?

A Pangolin is a beautiful, weird and endangered creature

What does a pangolin look like?



Some interesting qualaties of the pangolin:

  • Mammal with scales made of keratin (the same stuff as fingernails)
  • Bipedal despite hunching over looking like a quadraped
  • Diet consists of insects much like anteaters
  • Defense mechanism is to curl up into an armored ball.
